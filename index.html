How It Works
Your phone's accelerometer monitors G-force in real time. A sudden spike triggers an automatic 10-second countdown. If uncancelled, an SOS is sent.

What Gets Sent
Live GPS coordinates, name, blood group, allergies, nearest hospital details, and a Google Maps link — all in one SMS/WhatsApp message to your emergency contacts.
